Personal Statement Resources

Below are some excellent resources and annotated examples to help you prepare a great personal statement!

(1) https://lnkd.in/d_RFWbq

(2) https://lnkd.in/dawccfn

(3) https://lnkd.in/dgUjiUa

Note that the annotated statements are all very different, which is a good thing. There is no winning formula and you can write your statements in a way that best suits you and your experiences. These were helpful in informing me of which content to include and how I could most effectively make a case for admission.

In addition to statements, the CommKits offer resources for preparing cover letters, CVs, posters, talks and presentations, etc.
